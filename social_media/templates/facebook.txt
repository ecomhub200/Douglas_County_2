FACEBOOK BUSINESS PAGE POST GUIDELINES:

FORMAT:
- Optimal length: 100-250 characters for engagement (up to 63,206 allowed)
- Use images or videos -- posts with media get 2.3x more engagement
- Short, scannable text works best
- Use Facebook-native features (polls, events, live video)

TONE:
- Professional but warm and community-focused
- Accessible to non-technical audiences
- Storytelling approach when possible
- Encourage discussion and sharing

STRUCTURE TEMPLATE:
[Attention-grabbing opening line]

[Brief explanation or story -- 2-3 sentences max]

[CTA: Ask a question, invite to event, or share link]

[1-3 hashtags]

FACEBOOK-SPECIFIC TIPS:
- Video posts get 135% more organic reach than images
- Posts with questions get 100% more comments
- Keep text short -- Facebook truncates after 477 characters
- Link posts: Add commentary above the link, don't just share
- Best times: Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 1 PM EST
- Use Facebook Events for webinars and product launches
- Go Live for product demos (Facebook prioritizes live video)

CONTENT IDEAS FOR TRANSPORTATION:
- Share safety tips relevant to the community
- Post crash data visualizations with context
- Announce tool updates with demo videos
- Share industry news with your analysis
- Highlight community safety improvements

DO NOT:
- Post walls of text
- Use excessive hashtags (3 max)
- Be purely promotional -- provide value
- Ignore comments (Facebook penalizes unresponsive pages)
