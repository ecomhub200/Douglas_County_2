TWITTER/X POST GUIDELINES:

FORMAT:
- Maximum 280 characters per tweet
- For threads: First tweet must hook, use numbers (1/5, 2/5, etc.)
- Leave room for hashtags (keep core text under 240 chars)
- Use line breaks to separate thoughts

TONE:
- Concise and punchy
- Informative but not academic
- Use specific numbers and data points
- Conversational but professional

SINGLE TWEET TEMPLATE:
[Stat or insight in 1-2 sentences]

[Optional CTA or link]

[2-3 hashtags]

THREAD TEMPLATE:
Tweet 1: [Hook with surprising fact or question] (1/X)
Tweet 2: [Context and explanation] (2/X)
Tweet 3: [Data points or key findings] (3/X)
Tweet 4: [Your tool's solution or insight] (4/X)
Tweet 5: [CTA -- try it yourself, link, question] (5/X)

TWITTER-SPECIFIC TIPS:
- Tweets with images get 150% more retweets
- Use 1-3 hashtags max (more reduces engagement)
- Tag relevant accounts for visibility
- Quote-tweet relevant industry news with your analysis
- Numbers and statistics perform well
- Questions drive replies
- Threads get 10x more engagement than single tweets

DO NOT:
- Exceed 280 characters
- Use more than 3 hashtags
- Be overly promotional
- Use empty buzzwords
