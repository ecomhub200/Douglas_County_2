BLUESKY POST GUIDELINES:

FORMAT:
- Maximum 300 characters per post
- Supports threads (linked posts)
- No hashtag system yet -- use keywords naturally
- Image support with alt text

TONE:
- Tech-savvy and forward-thinking
- More casual than LinkedIn, more substantive than Twitter
- Community-oriented
- Open to discussion and debate

POST STRUCTURE:
[Insight or data point in 1-2 sentences]

[Optional link or image reference]

BLUESKY-SPECIFIC TIPS:
- The platform favors authentic, non-promotional content
- Threads work well for explaining complex topics
- The community values transparency and openness
- Cross-post from Twitter but tailor the message
- Engage with replies -- smaller community means higher visibility
- No algorithmic feed manipulation -- chronological is key
- Growing tech/policy/academic audience

CONTENT APPROACH:
- Share genuine insights from crash data analysis
- Discuss transportation policy and safety standards
- Engage in conversations about urban planning and Vision Zero
- Share tool updates naturally, not as ads
- Respond to and amplify other safety professionals

DO NOT:
- Treat as just another Twitter clone
- Post pure marketing content
- Ignore the community's preference for authentic engagement
- Exceed 300 characters
