DEFAULT SOCIAL MEDIA POST GUIDELINES:

FORMAT:
- Keep within the platform's character limit
- Use clear, scannable formatting
- Include a call-to-action
- Add relevant hashtags (within platform limits)

TONE:
- Professional but approachable
- Data-driven when possible
- Genuine and helpful
- Not overly promotional

STRUCTURE:
[Hook: Attention-grabbing first line]

[Body: 2-3 sentences of value -- insight, data, or story]

[CTA: Clear next step for the reader]

[Hashtags: Platform-appropriate number]

UNIVERSAL RULES:
1. Every image must have alt text (WCAG 2.1 AA)
2. Verify all statistics before posting
3. Never be insensitive about crash victims
4. Maintain professional tone while being engaging
5. Include a clear call-to-action
6. Use data to back up claims
7. Stay relevant to traffic safety professionals
