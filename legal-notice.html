Legal Notice & Data Disclaimer

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

This page contains important legal notices and disclaimers regarding the use of crash data within CRASH LENS. Please read this information carefully before using the platform.

1. Data Source

The crash data used in CRASH LENS is sourced from Virginia Roads, maintained by the Virginia Department of Transportation (VDOT). The primary source of this data is owned and maintained by the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV).

2. Title 23 USC Section 409 - Federal Protection

Critical Legal Notice

Under Title 23 United States Code, Section 409, crash information compiled or collected for highway safety purposes cannot be:

  • Used in discovery in any Federal or State court proceeding
  • Admitted as evidence in any Federal or State court proceeding
  • Considered for other purposes in any action for damages arising from any occurrence at a location mentioned or addressed in such reports, surveys, schedules, lists, or data

This protection applies to all crash data, analysis results, and reports generated by CRASH LENS against VDOT, the Commonwealth of Virginia, or any local jurisdiction.

2.1 Purpose of Section 409

This federal law was enacted to encourage states to identify and improve hazardous locations without fear that their efforts to collect safety data would be used against them in litigation. The law ensures that highway safety programs can collect comprehensive data to improve roadway safety.
