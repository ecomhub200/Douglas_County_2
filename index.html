Powerful Features

A comprehensive crash analysis platform built on proven engineering algorithms, FHWA methodologies, and MUTCD standards — with optional intelligent assistants for deeper insights

Core Analysis Engine

Crash Data Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of crash patterns, trends, and contributing factors with interactive visualizations and EPDO scoring algorithms.

Hotspot Identification

Identify high-risk intersections and corridors using advanced geospatial analysis, heat mapping, and route-based EPDO ranking algorithms.

MUTCD Signal Warrant Analysis

Automated signal warrant evaluation based on MUTCD standards with detailed warrant compliance documentation and recommendations.

FHWA Countermeasure Matching

Automatically match crash patterns to proven FHWA safety countermeasures with Crash Modification Factors (CMFs) from the CMF Clearinghouse.

Report Generation

Generate professional PDF and Word reports for stakeholders, HSIP grant applications, and public meetings with automated data summaries.

HSIP Grant Support

Automated location ranking, benefit-cost analysis, and crash data justification for Highway Safety Improvement Program applications.

Safety Focus Areas

Pedestrian Safety

Analyze pedestrian crash patterns by time-of-day, lighting conditions, and location to identify RRFB and crosswalk improvement opportunities.

Bicycle Safety

Evaluate bike lane placements, right-hook crash patterns, and protected intersection designs with dedicated bicycle crash analysis.

Intersection Safety

Deep-dive analysis of intersection crash patterns including angle, rear-end, and left-turn crashes with signal timing evaluation.

School Zone Safety

Identify crash patterns near school zones with proximity analysis and school-hour crash filtering for targeted safety improvements.

Transit Safety

Analyze crashes involving transit vehicles, bus stops, and transit corridors to improve multimodal transportation safety.

Crash Trend Predictions

Identify emerging crash patterns and trend forecasting using historical data analysis and statistical modeling algorithms.

Advanced Tools & Automation

Before & After Study

Compare crash data before and after safety improvements with statistical significance testing to measure countermeasure effectiveness.

Automatic Email Reporting

Schedule automated crash reports delivered to your inbox — weekly summaries, monthly digests, or custom schedules based on your preferences.

Automatic Grant Notifications

Get notified when new HSIP or safety grant opportunities match your jurisdiction's crash patterns and improvement needs.

Traffic Asset Analysis

Correlate crash data with roadway infrastructure — guardrails, signage, pavement markings, and signal equipment for asset-based safety planning.

Multi-Level Jurisdictions

Analyze crash data at state, district, MPO, county, and city levels with configurable jurisdiction boundaries and cross-region comparisons.

Optional Smart Assistants

Five specialized assistants available on-demand for deeper analysis — can be toggled on or off based on your team's preference. All core features work independently.
