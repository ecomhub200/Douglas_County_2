Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after the free trial?

After 14 days, you can choose a paid plan to continue. Your data and settings are preserved when you upgrade.

Can I use my own API key?

Yes! All plans support "bring your own key" for unlimited AI queries using your Claude API key.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and can arrange invoicing for Agency plans.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
