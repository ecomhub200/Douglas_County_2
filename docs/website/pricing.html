Frequently asked questions

What are credits and how do they work?

Credits are used for AI-powered features like document generation, compatibility scoring, and counselor sessions. Different features use different amounts of credits, and your plan includes a monthly allocation that refreshes each billing cycle.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, our Free plan is available indefinitely with limited features. You can upgrade to a paid plan whenever you're ready to access more features and credits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and for annual plans, we also support wire transfers.

Is there a discount for students?

Yes, we offer special pricing for students with valid .edu email addresses. Please contact our support team for details.
